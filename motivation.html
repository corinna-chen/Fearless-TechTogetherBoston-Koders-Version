AFFIRMATIONS

Positive affirmations are statements that can help you overcome negative thoughts and self-sabotaging. They are very powerful because they relieve you of any negativity, anxiety, worry, and fear. When these affirmations are repeated, you begin believing in them. This can change your pattern of thinking filling you with the courage and confidence required to make positive changes in your life.
