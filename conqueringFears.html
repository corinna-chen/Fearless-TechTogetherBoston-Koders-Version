Conquering Fears is Harder Than Just Getting Over It

It's a long process but well worth it. Here are some starting steps!

- Identifying why you have this fear

- Ask yourself is this is a fear that is irrational and if not, how can you prevent this fear from happening?

- Can you handle the process of overcoming this fear?

- Do you have a support system to help you through this fear?

- What will you get out of overcoming this fear?

Treatments and Strategies to Overcome Fears

- Meditation

- Exposure Therapy

- Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

- Counseling and/or Therapy

Arachnophobia

Fear of Spiders

Expose yourself to spiders! Start with stuffed animals, halloween spiders, watch videos of spiders, and observe spiders from a distance. From there, you can try to capture a spider in your house when comfortable.

Acrophobia

Fear of height

Start low and work your way up. Start on the bottom and set goals to make it higher up each time. Try to be high up in a safe structure such as a building.

Aerophobia

Fear of Flying

Knowledge is power! Learn about how safe planes are, safety procedures, built in safety mechanisms, etc. Talking to flight attendants, pilots, frequent flyers, or therapists.

Astraphobia

Fear of Thunder and Lightning

Slowly expose yourself to thunder and lightning. Begin by wearing headphones and progressivly turn down the volume, exposing yourself to the sounds of storms.

Autophobia

Fear of Being Alone

Spend good quality time with yourself! Talk to people whether it be a friend, family, or a stranger. It's important to make deep connections with people. Get a pet! Pets are very loyal and provide companionship.

Claustrophobia

Fear of Confined or Crowded Spaces

Exposure therpay may help to gradually put you in confined or crowded spaces. Virtual reality simulates the environment in a safe space. Having a support system is important when walking through crowded places or going into a confined space.

Cynophobia

Fear of Dogs

CBT combined with exposure therapy is very effective. CBT allows for one to read a dogs behavior and interpret it correctly. You can increase the amount of exposure you have with dogs until you feel comfortable around them.

Entomophobia

Fear of Insects

Exposure therapy! Talk about insects, watch videos, dig in the dirt for them, etc! Spend lot's of time outdoors and see how they aren't a threat to you. Learn about the insects in your region.
